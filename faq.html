Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about WorkHub

What is WorkHub?

WorkHub is a professional freelancing platform that connects businesses with talented freelancers worldwide. We offer a secure, easy-to-use platform for hiring and working with professionals across various industries.

How does WorkHub work?

It's simple! Clients post projects or browse freelancer services, freelancers submit proposals or offer services, and once hired, they collaborate through our platform. We handle payments securely and provide tools for communication and project management.

Is WorkHub free to use?

Yes, creating an account and browsing services is completely free. We charge a small service fee (5%) only when transactions are completed successfully.

How do I hire a freelancer?

Browse our services, review freelancer profiles and ratings, then place an order or send a custom request. You can communicate with freelancers before hiring to ensure they're the right fit for your project.

How do I know if a freelancer is qualified?

All freelancers have detailed profiles showing their skills, experience, portfolio, and client reviews. We also verify freelancer identities and track their performance on our platform.

What if I'm not satisfied with the work?

We offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with the delivered work, you can request revisions or contact our support team for dispute resolution. In extreme cases, we may provide a full refund.

How do I get started as a freelancer?

Create your freelancer account, complete your profile with skills and portfolio, then start creating services or bidding on projects. The more complete your profile, the more likely you are to attract clients.

How much can I earn on WorkHub?

Earnings vary based on your skills, experience, and the services you offer. Many freelancers earn anywhere from $500 to $5000+ per month. Top performers can earn significantly more.

How do I set my prices?

You have full control over your pricing. Research similar services on our platform, consider your experience level, and price competitively. You can always adjust your prices as you gain more experience and positive reviews.

How do payments work?

We use a secure escrow system. Clients pay upfront, funds are held securely, and released to freelancers upon successful completion of work. This protects both parties.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through encrypted channels.

When do freelancers get paid?

Freelancers receive payment once the client approves the delivered work or after a 7-day auto-approval period. Payments are typically processed within 1-3 business days.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our support team through live chat, email at support@workhub.com, or by submitting a ticket through your dashboard. We typically respond within 24 hours.

What are your support hours?

Our support team is available 24/7 for urgent issues. For general inquiries, our standard support hours are Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM EST.

Do you offer phone support?

Phone support is available for Pro and Enterprise plan subscribers. Basic plan users can access support through chat and email.

Still have questions?

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Our support team is here to help.
